Privacy Policy for Quran Unlock

Last Updated: October 29, 2024

Introduction

Quran Unlock ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

Information We Collect

Analytics Data

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. Firebase may automatically collect the following information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
  • Usage Data: App features used, screens viewed, session duration, app launches, button clicks, and interactions within the App
  • Performance Data: App launch time, hang rate, energy usage, and other technical performance metrics
  • Crash Data: Information about app crashes and errors to help us fix bugs and improve stability

Local Data (Stored Only on Your Device)

The following data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Your Quran reading progress and history
  • Apps you've selected to block
  • Your daily reading schedule and preferences
  • Streak information and reading statistics
  • App usage patterns and blocking history

We do not have access to this information. It remains private on your device.

How We Use Information

We use the collected analytics data to:

  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Understand which features are most useful to our users
  • Measure app stability and reliability
  • Enhance user experience and develop new features
  • Analyze usage patterns to make the app better

We do not:

  • Sell your information to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Share your personal information with other companies for their marketing purposes
  • Access or transmit your Quran reading data or blocked apps list
  • Collect or transmit information about which apps you use or when you use them

Data Sharing

Third-Party Service Providers

We use Google Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google LLC. Firebase processes data according to Google's Privacy Policy, which can be found at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Firebase may share anonymized, aggregated data with Google for analytics purposes. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

Screen Time and App Usage Data

Data about which apps you've selected to block, when they're blocked, and your app usage patterns is never collected, transmitted, or shared. This information remains completely private on your device. We use Screen Time API (iOS) and Usage Access/Accessibility Services (Android) solely to provide the core app blocking functionality, not to collect or analyze your data.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

Data Retention

  • Analytics Data: Retained by Firebase according to their data retention policies (typically 2-14 months)
  • Local Device Data: Remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we collect
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-Out: Limit ad tracking in your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking; Android: Settings > Google > Ads)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our App may be used by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. The analytics data we collect is anonymous and not linked to any individual's identity.

App Permissions

The App requires the following permissions to function:

iOS - Screen Time API

Required to block and unblock selected apps. This permission is used solely for the core functionality of the App.

Android - Usage Access / Accessibility Services

Required to detect when blocked apps are opened and to enforce app restrictions.

Important: These permissions do not transmit data about your app usage to our servers. All blocking and unblocking happens locally on your device.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through our App. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from the laws of your country. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending a notification through the App (for material changes)

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: kalycodes@gmail.com

GitHub: https://github.com/kalycoding

Additional Information for California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to request deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

We do not sell personal information.

Additional Information for EU/UK Residents

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

Legal Basis for Processing: We process your data based on:

  • Your consent (for analytics)
  • Legitimate interests (app functionality and improvement)

Summary

What we collect: Anonymous analytics data (device info, app interactions, crash reports)

What we DON'T collect: Your Quran reading data, blocked apps list, app usage patterns, or any personally identifiable information

Where your data goes: Only Firebase Analytics (Google) - and it's anonymous

Your control: All sensitive data stays on your device. You can opt-out of tracking in device settings.
